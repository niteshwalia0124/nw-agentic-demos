Policy: Arogya Sanjeevani Standard Health Policy
Company: SecureLife Insurance

1. Coverage Details:
- This policy covers hospitalization expenses for illness or injury.
- Room rent is covered up to 2% of sum insured per day (max Rs. 5000).
- ICU expenses are covered up to 5% of sum insured per day (max Rs. 10000).
- AYUSH Treatment: Expenses incurred for inpatient care treatment under Ayurveda, Yoga and Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha and Homeopathy systems of medicines are covered up to 100% of Sum Insured.

2. Specific Covers:
- Cataract treatment is covered up to 25% of sum insured or Rs. 40,000, whichever is lower, per eye.
- Pre-hospitalization expenses are covered for 30 days prior to admission.
- Post-hospitalization expenses are covered for 60 days after discharge.

3. Exclusions:
- Dental treatment or surgery of any kind unless requiring hospitalization due to an accident.
- Cosmetic or plastic surgery unless required due to injury or accident.
- Rest cure, rehabilitation, and respite care.
